HathiTrust Research Center

The HathiTrust Research Center (HTRC) facilitates computational text and data mining of the HathiTrust corpus for nonprofit and educational users.

The HTRC is a collaborative research center launched jointly by Indiana University and the University of Illinois, along with HathiTrust. Leveraging data storage and computational infrastructure at Indiana University and the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, the HTRC develops cutting-edge software tools and cyberinfrastructure to enable advanced computational access to the growing digital record of human knowledge.
